Experiment about Quality of Servie(QoS), Quality of Experience(QoE) and recommendations

Welcome to our brief experiment on quality-of-experience.

In this experiment, you will watch movie sessions, and after each session, comprising a pair of movies, you will be asked to assess which movie of the pair you preferred the most.

Some disturbances may occur in the middle of the streaming of some movies. They were inserted on purpose.

The experiment will take around 5 minutes.

The experiment works on Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome, but NOT on Safari.
